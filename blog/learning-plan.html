Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I am hoping to use the skills I gain at EDA to help individuals, families, and communities through the positive use of internet-related technologies. As I am also a primary caregiver to a toddler, I do not have a grand-destination in mind regarding my next career step - the goal is more holistic. I would like to find flexible employment that will allow me to continue to grow my tech skills while engaging my experience in “helping” skills.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My biggest strength is that I like fast moving environments that involve creative ideation and teamwork. My technical skills will be a weakness, which will intensify if I focus on this element of my skills too much and measure my contribution to the group based on coding skills alone. There will probably be some instances where I am embarrassed by my level of technical skills, it will important that I don't let this emotional reaction interfere with my learning too much.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I expect that I will feel quite overwhelmed at the end of each week. This feeling may build up and start to erode my ability to concentrate. I will have to make an effort to be aware of my own internal state and need for timeout.

What non-technical skills (human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Can I say all of them?

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I really just expect what you have outlined that you will provide. In saying this, I know that the next few weeks will be difficult and there may be times when the team can’t provide 100% of the usual support. I understand that we will have individual support to help us with developing our core skills and that this is scheduled. I also understand that there will be technical support - but again this is limited to what the team can provide based on BAU - not my whims and fancies.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

My expectation is that I make the most of my time and take full advantage of this unique opportunity. This means making an effort to understand everyone around me and to work with them in a positive and productive manner. It means that I will have to manage myself well through self-care. There are probably going to be a few times when I feel that things have moved faster than I would have liked - especially technical lessons. Foundations has taught me that there are caring people around who will help me get up to speed provided I ask for help in a positive way.

Based on your knowledge of Bootcamp, how and where will you seek help in a timely way?

I understand that I will have a human skills facilitator with whom I will have a 1:1 every second week to support my emotional well-being. I know that EDA has a counselling service for students so should I need urgent emotional support for some reason, I will ask to be connected to this service or something else deemed relevant. Regarding support with technical skills, I will keep note of my technical skills concerns discuss them with the person leading my Tech-in at the end of each technical phase. I will reflect on my technical and emotional concerns at the start of each week for 20 minutes and update my learning plan and focus for the week - both technical and emotional.

My learning plan 🎓

Long-term career goal

Be a self-employed web development consultant or work for a company that advocates for a better internet!

Learning strengths and limitations

I am very resilient and I can stay calm if I have to start again - better to redo work than do it all wrong! I am creative and I enjoy problem-solving and trying to learn about computers and the web! A weakness is that I like to consider things deeply and there isn't always time.

Human skills I would like to develop at EDA

Self-compassion, teamwork, and communication

How I will manage my workload

I am treating my study like a full-time job. I work set hours (40+ per week) and take breaks.

Actions I will take if things are building up and getting to me

I will book an appointment with my facilitator to make a plan, if required. But going for a walk and having some food and water is a good first step!

How and where I will seek help in a timely way

I will reach out for help in the foundations channel if I am stuck on a problem for over 20 minutes. If that isn’t working then I will contact my facilitator for one-to-one help.

What I expect from the facilitation team

Support in Slack Channels, one-to-ones, and understanding of my learning differences and strengths.

Scheduling info and other commitments

I have very good availability between 10am to 2:30pm during foundations. I am also available after 8pm when my toddler is a sleep. I know that I have to be available for the set hours during Bootcamp. I have an all day appointment on Monday 14 February 2022.
